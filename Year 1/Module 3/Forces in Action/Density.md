Density is a quantity that is constant for a specific material, regardless of it's mass or volume. 

Density can be found using:
$$\begin{align}
 & \rho = \frac{m}{V}
\end{align}$$
Where $\rho$ is density, $m$ is the mass and $V$ is volume.
