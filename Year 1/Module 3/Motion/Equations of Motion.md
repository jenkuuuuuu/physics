
| Symbol | Quantity              | S.I. Unit                     |
| ------ | --------------------- | ----------------------------- |
| s      | displacement          | metre (m)                     |
| u      | velocity at the start | metres per second (ms^-1^)    |
| v      | velocity at the end   | metres per second (ms^-1^)    |
| a      | acceleration          | metres per second^2^ (ms^-2^) |
| t      | time interval         | second (s)                    |
$$
\begin{align}
 & v = u + at \\
  & \\ \\

 & s = ut + \frac{1}{2}at^2 \\
 \\ \\

 & v^{2} = u^2+2as \\ \\
 \\

 & s=\frac{1}{2}(u+v)t
\end{align}$$
